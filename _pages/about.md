Hey all, I am Prathmesh. I have a total of 10+ years of experience in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Currently, I work as Vice President of Machine Learning at [InFoCusp Innovations Pvt. Ltd.](http://www.infocusp.com/). Here, I lead projects not limiting to Prompt Engineering, RAG, LLM fine-tuning, Agentic AI, and Evals. I also work on the research problems in computer vision around hardware acceleration and optimization. The business domains that I have worked on so far are Health, Recruitment, User research and survey, and fishing industry. 

I hold a PhD from the [Chair of Pattern Recognition](https://lme.tf.fau.de/), FAU, Germany. My dissertation was on understanding and analyzing scenes in the field of digital humanities, with a major focus on Art-history, Classical Archaeology and Christian Archaeology. Here, I applied existing state-of-the art methods to understand and link the artworks using object detection, pose estimation and scene compositions. 

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a Machine learning Engineer at [InFoCusp Innovations Pvt. Ltd.](http://www.infocusp.com/), where I worked upon various projects involving research and development of products based on client requirements powered by ML. My role was to develop and test algorithms, mentor interns for the projects involving CV and natural language processing (NLP). Before leaving, I was also leading the in-house research project which focused on generating music sequences using deep learning. You can find the details of the projects [here](cv)
